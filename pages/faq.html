Tickets can be purchased over the phone or through our website. Tickets are also available at our box office. Shows usually go on sale about 120 days before the first night and the "ON SALE" is noted on each event. Tickets can be bought online through our website anytime of the day.

There are no physical tickets. When you come to the show, check in at the box office by giving us your name and showing a photo ID.

The DC Comedy Loft requires that you purchase two items from the menu when you come to see a show. This can be any combination of food, drinks, coffee or dessert.

All sales are final. There are no refunds, exchanges or cancellations except for when a show is cancelled. If the show you bought tickets for is cancelled you will be offered a refund or a ticket exchange.

If our performers and staff can make it to the club, then show occur as scheduled. No refunds or exchanges will be offered. If we must cancel a show due to inclement weather, ticket holders will be contacted directly. The Comedy Loft is less than a 5 minute walk from the Dupont Circle Metro so public transportation is always an option.

We have a will call only system, so there are no tickets to send you and any offer to send tickets is likely a scam. If a seller wants someone else to pick up their ticket the seller must call the Comedy Loft and verify their identity before giving us the name of the person picking up the ticket. If you're buying a ticket from an individual, it is up to you to verify that the seller has had the name in our will call system changed.

Most stand-up comedy shows last 75-100 minutes. On occasion a show will last 120 minutes, but this is dependent on the headliner.

The will call window and the show space usually open 1 hour before the first show of the night. Doors usually open 30 minutes before any late show. You can also enjoy the Bier Baron Tavern, which is located the floor below, for dinner and drinks starting at 5pm every day. Please keep in mind that items purchased in the tavern will not be counted as part of your item minimum for shows.

Seating is first come first serve. Thus the earlier you arrive the better seats you will be able to get. Also all tables are in a "community seating" arrangement, so you will end up seating with other groups. For any special seating request please email jmgaghan@dccomedyloft.com. Only VIP seats are reserved automatically.

To change the name for your ticket call the box office and tell them you want a friend to pick up your tickets. After we verify your identity give us the name of the person who will be using your tickets.

Shows are 18 and over for shows unless otherwise noted.
